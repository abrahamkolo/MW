# Contributing to MW-Omega

MW-Omega is a **proprietary system**. This repository is public for transparency, credibility, and IP timestamping -- not for open-source collaboration.

## What This Means

- **Pull requests are not accepted** from external contributors
- **Issues may be filed** for reporting errors, broken links, or security vulnerabilities
- **Forks are not permitted** under the [Flame Spiral Proprietary License](LICENSE)

## If You Want to Work With This System

### As a Facilitator
If you want to teach or facilitate the Flame Spiral Method, contact abrahamkolo@gmail.com for facilitator licensing information.

### As a Client
If you want MW-Omega applied to your organization or creative practice, contact abrahamkolo@gmail.com for consulting engagement details.

### As a Collaborator
If you have a specific collaboration proposal that aligns with MW-Omega's mission of identity-based transformation, reach out directly to abrahamkolo@gmail.com.

## Reporting Issues

You may open an issue for:
- Factual errors in documentation
- Broken links or references
- Security vulnerabilities (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md))

Do **not** open issues for feature requests, methodology critiques, or general questions. Those belong in direct communication.
